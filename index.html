FAQ
Common Questions
What kind of deals does Alethinx source?+
Alethinx covers both business acquisitions (healthcare, IT/MSP, B2B services, F&B) and commercial real estate (NNN, industrial, self-storage, medical office). All deals are analyzed for passive income potential with zero personal guarantee financing.
How does the AI Board Analysis work?+
Every deal is analyzed by a 5-member AI advisory board: CEO (strategy), CFO (financials), CPA (tax structure), Risk Analyst (downside), and Legal (deal structure). Each provides a specialized analysis, then a synthesized Board Verdict gives a go/no-go recommendation — all in under 60 seconds.
What does "zero personal guarantee" mean?+
Traditional business acquisitions often require you to personally guarantee the debt. Alethinx structures deals using combinations of SBA loans, seller financing, and earn-outs that minimize or eliminate personal financial risk — so your personal assets are never on the line.
Can I try it before paying?+
Yes. The free tier includes 2 sample deals with limited AI analysis, plus full access to market data. No credit card required. Upgrade to Pro ($49/mo) when you're ready for the full deal pipeline and unlimited AI Board analysis.
Who is Alethinx for?+
First-time acquirers, search fund operators, independent sponsors, and CRE investors who want institutional-grade deal intelligence without hiring a full advisory team. If you're targeting $250K+ in passive income through business or property acquisition, Alethinx is built for you.
Why $124 trillion? What's the market opportunity?+
According to Cerulli Associates and McKinsey, the Baby Boomer generation will transfer $124 trillion in wealth by 2048. 6 million small businesses need new owners by 2035. Currently 92% of business exits end in closure — not sale. This is the largest ownership transition in modern history, and Alethinx is built to help individual buyers participate in it.
